# Prompt: Promote notes into a synthesis

Combine 2+ notes (and optionally other syntheses) into a `synthesis/` entry.

1. Pick the driving `question:`.
2. `research-vault capture --type synthesis --title "..."` then set `contributing_ids: [...]` (note/source/synthesis ids — recursive synthesis allowed).
3. Synthesize across sources: agreements, tensions, what would change your mind.
4. `stage: synthesized` (or `stable` once durable). Validate all `contributing_ids` resolve.
